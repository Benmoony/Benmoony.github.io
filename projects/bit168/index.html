ABOUT

Hello and welcome to my website. This website was built by me as a portfolio for my work in my BIT 168 class at Cascadia College. Here I experimented with the use of animations, proper design layout, and a little bit of responsive design. This website is a real first step into the world of web design, and I am incredibly excited to continue down this path and make a career out of it. Though all of the navigation links lead somewhere, the News section is very barren and filled to the brim with lorem ipsum due to time constraints. I thank you for reading and take this as a showcase of my beginning steps into the world of Interactive Media Design.

Me

I was born in Boston, Massachusetts and was raised in Seattle Washington during my childhood. After attending the Bush School for highschool, I went to the Schulich School of Music in Montreal, Quebec for two years as a bassoon performance major. However I decided to change career paths and came back home to study programming and web design at Cascadia College and am hoping to transfer into the UW Bothell IMD program. My current career aspirations are to work within the Video Game Industry or the Web Industry and create experiences that shape lives and give people lasting impression on their soul.
