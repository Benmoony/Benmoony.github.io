Lavender, Right Hand of the Sun

Lavender is the eternal student of the Sun God. Learned in the ways of arcane power, she has heard tell of a prophecy which the Sun God has chosen to ignore. It is up to her to uncover what is happening to her world and to bring light to the darkness
