Lady Lucid, Stalker and Spy Extraordinaire

This elusive Lady is an assassin and spy for the Mooncult. Deft in the art of killing and reconnaissance, she has been instructed by her order to tail Lavender and follow her trail. If our hero gets wind of the going's on behind the scene's, Lady Lucid will be their to silence those inquisitive minds.
