Oldfashion

Ingredients:

  • 2 teaspoons simple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • 2 dashes bitters
  • ice cubes
  • 1-2 ounces of bourbon whiskey
  • 1 sliced orange
  • garnish of choice

Directions

  1. In oldfashioned glass, combine syrup and bitters
  2. Fill flass halfway with ice & stir about a dozen times
  3. Add enough ice to fill the glass
  4. Squeeze orange peel over glass to extract oils, and peel to glass
  5. Add Whiskey, stir about a dozen times until drink is cold and alcohol has softened
  6. Garnish with Cherry or garnish of choice
